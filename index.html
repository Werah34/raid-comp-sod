What is this site all about?

The purpose of WoW Raid Comp is to help visualize the composition of a 10 or 25 person raid.

How do i use this?

• Drag a Specialization to a raid position.
   Alternatively "Click" on a specialization and the first available empty spot will be filled.

• Drag a Specialization outside of the raid grid to remove it.
   Alternatively "Double Click" on a specialization in the raid grid and it will be removed.

• "Right-Click" on a Specialization to see a list of Buffs/Debuffs that it provides.

• Share a Link or an Image of the "Perfect" WoW Raid Comp.

Missing Buffs/Debuffs??

The scope of the site is primarily focused on Damage increasing Buffs and Debuffs with some Utility.
(buffs such as auras / totems that increase "Resistances" and "Armor" for example are not included.)
